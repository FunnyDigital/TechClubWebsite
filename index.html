About Us

The Tech Club is a vibrant community for students passionate about technology. Our mission is to provide a platform for learning, sharing knowledge, and working on exciting tech projects.

Whether you're a seasoned coder, a budding robotics engineer, or just curious about how things work, our club offers something for everyone. We believe in hands-on learning and collaborative problem-solving.
